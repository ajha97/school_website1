School Church

The church in our school is a big and silent place. The students visit there before entering the school or after the school has ended and the students are waiting for their parents, but on some festivals we have to come and attend the function.



School Library

Was established in 1980

Our school library is a nice and silent place, where we can sit and read books without any disturbance. All the teachers and students obey the rules of being silent. there are four book shelves and four shelves are attached to the wall.



School Auditorium

Was established in 1982

Our auditorium is very big and it has a big stage where we attend the assembly and all the performances are held on the stage like recitation ,singing and dancing competitions. There is also a large screen that is used for quizes and debates.
